# ⚡ E-Watt Technical Specification

## 1. Thermodynamic Anchor
1 E-Watt ($SOV) = 1 kWh of verifiable energy generated by the Bitopia Orbital Grid.

## 2. The Tokenomic Loop
- **Minting:** $SOV is minted only when a StarNode cryptographically verifies solar capture.
- **Burning:** $SOV is burned to access computation, storage shards, or orbital transit.
- **Sustainability:** The "Knowledge Sprint" mechanism lowers tx-fees for energy-efficient innovation.

## 3. RWA Integration
Every E-Watt is tied to a specific solar array in the **Galactic Registry**. 
- **Asset ID:** `BTP-E-XXX`
- **Verification:** Proof-of-Efficiency (PoE) consensus.
